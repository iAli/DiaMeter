About DiaMeter

DiaMeter is a graduation project submitted to College of Computer and Information Technology, Shaqra University in partial fulfillment of the requirements for the degree of Bachelor in Computer Science.

The project aims to take advantage of the latest technologies to provide a health service that helps in combating diabetes. It takes advantage of machine learning techniques to predict the risk of developing diabetes. In addition, the system offers a follow-up option for diabetics and provide advice according to each case.
